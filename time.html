Time

Explore Xhosa time expressions and months with Sazi and his cousins! Type the term, check your answer, or reveal it to hear how it sounds.

Name Description When Used Pronunciation
January EyoMqungu
Month of the Tambuki Grass, marking the start of the year. Inyanga yengca yaseTambuki, eqala unyaka. First month of the year. Inyanga yokuqala yonyaka.
February EyoMdumba
Month of the grain harvest. Inyanga yokuqokelela amabele. Second month of the year. Inyanga yesibini yonyaka.
March EyoKwindla
Month of the first fruits, a time for harvests. Inyanga yeziqhamo zokuqala, ixesha lokuvuna. Third month of the year. Inyanga yesithathu yonyaka.
April uTshaz’iimpuzi
Month of the fading pumpkins. Inyanga yamathanga ashwabanisayo. Fourth month of the year. Inyanga yesine yonyaka.
May EyeCanzibe
Month of the Canopus star, signaling cooler weather. Inyanga yenkanyezi yaseCanzibe, ebonisa imozulu epholileyo. Fifth month of the year. Inyanga yesihlanu yonyaka.
June EyeSilimela
Month of the Pleiades star cluster. Inyanga yeqela lenkanyezi laseSilimela. Sixth month of the year. Inyanga yesithandathu yonyaka.
July EyeKhala
Month of the aloe bloom. Inyanga yokuqhakaza kwekhala. Seventh month of the year. Inyanga yesixhenxe yonyaka.
August EyeThupha
Month of the budding plants. Inyanga yezityalo ezithomblayo. Eighth month of the year. Inyanga yesibhozo yonyaka.
September EyoMsintsi
Month of the spring blossoms. Inyanga yeentyatyambo zentwasahlobo. Ninth month of the year. Inyanga yesithoba yonyaka.
October EyeDwarha
Month of the flowering plants. Inyanga yezityalo eziqhakazayo. Tenth month of the year. Inyanga yeshumi yonyaka.
November EyeNkanga
Month of the small birds. Inyanga yeentaka ezincinci. Eleventh month of the year. Inyanga yeshumi elinanye yonyaka.
December EyoMnga
Month of the acacia trees. Inyanga yemithi yeminga. Twelfth month of the year. Inyanga yeshumi elinambini yonyaka.
Monday UMvulo
The first day of the week. Usuku lokuqala lweveki. First day after Sunday. Usuku lokuqala emva kweCawe.
Morning Kusasa
The start of the day, when the sun rises. Ukuqala kosuku, xa ilanga liphuma. Early part of the day. Inxalenye yokuqala yosuku.
Summer Ihlobo
The warm season of the year. Ixesha elishushu lonyaka. Warm months of the year. Iinyanga ezishushu zonyaka.
