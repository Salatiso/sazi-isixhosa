Activities

Join Sazi and his cousins in fun Xhosa games and activities! Type the activity name, check your answer, or reveal it to play along.

Name Description When/Where Played Pronunciation
Telling Folk Tales Ukuxoxa Iintsomi
At night, elders tell children folk tales that teach morals and pass down culture. Ngobusuku, abantu abadala bahlala bexoxa iintsomi kubantwana. Ezi ntsomi zifundisa izifundo zokuziphatha kwaye zidlulisela ilifa lenkcubeko. At night, after dinner. Ngobusuku, emva kwesidlo sangokuhlwa.
Skipping Rope Umgusha
A game played with a long rope swung by two people, with one jumping over it. Umdlalo odlalwa ngentambo ende ejikeleziswa ngabantu ababini, omnye umntu atsho agxume phezu kwayo. During playtime in open spaces. Ngexesha lokudlala emini, kuyo nayiphi na indawo evulekileyo.
Ankle Races Ukugijima Ngeqakala
A game where children race with their ankles tied together with rope or grass, aiming to run a distance without falling. Umdlalo apho abantwana bagijima beqhina amaqakala abo ngentambo okanye ingca. Injongo kukugijima umgama othile ngaphandle kokuwa okanye kokuqhawuka kwentambo. During playtime in open spaces. Ngexesha lokudlala emini, kwiindawo ezivulekileyo.
Playing with Clay Ukudlala Ngodongwe
Children use clay to create various figures like cows, houses, or people, encouraging creativity. Abantwana basebenzisa udongwe ukwenza imifanekiso eyahlukeneyo, njengeenkomo, izindlu, okanye abantu. Lo msebenzi ukhuthaza ubuchule nokucinga. During playtime in areas with clay. Ngexesha lokudlala emini, kwiindawo ezinodongwe.
Sliding on the Ground Ukutyibiliza Emhlabeni
Children sit on a flat or sloped surface and slide down, sometimes using large leaves or a small board. Abantwana bahlala phantsi kwindawo ethe tyaba okanye esecaleni kwenduli encinci baze batyibilize phantsi. Ngamanye amaxesha basebenzisa amagqabi amakhulu okanye ibhodi encinci ukutyibiliza kamnandi. During playtime in suitable areas. Ngexesha lokudlala emini, kwiindawo ezifanelekileyo.
Playing with Sticks Ukudlala Izinti
Children use small sticks to play various games, such as tossing or shaping them into animals or cars. Abantwana basebenzisa izinti ezincinci ukudlala imidlalo eyahlukeneyo, enokubandakanya ukuziphosa, ukuzigxumisa, okanye ukuzenza izinto ezahlukeneyo (njengezilwanyana okanye iimoto). During playtime anywhere. Ngexesha lokudlala emini, kuyo nayiphi na indawo.
Traditional Songs and Dances Ukudlala Iingoma Nemidaniso Yesintu
Children learn traditional songs and dances, sometimes playing drums or toy instruments. Abantwana bafunda iingoma zesintu nemidaniso, abanye baphathe izigubhu okanye amanye amathoyi okwenza umculo. During community gatherings or playtime. Ngamaxesha ahlukeneyo, kwiintlanganiso zoluntu okanye nje ngexesha lokudlala.
Morabaraba Diketo/Morabaraba
A strategic board game played with 24 stones on a patterned board, aiming to capture the opponent’s stones. Umdlalo webhodi wamasu odlalwa ngamatye amabini aneshumi elinambini kwibhodi enemigca ecwangcisiweyo. Injongo kukubamba amatye omchasi. During leisure time, often by men and elders. Ngamaxesha olonwabo, ngamadoda nabakhulu.
Stick Fighting Intonga
A men’s game where two people fight with long sticks, showcasing strength and skill. Umdlalo wamadoda apho abantu ababini belwa ngentonga ende. Lo mdlalo ubonisa ubugorha, amandla, nobuchule. During special occasions or celebrations, by men. Ngamaxesha akhethekileyo okanye kwimibhiyozo, ngamadoda.
Traditional Music Umculo Wesintu
Music plays a key role in Xhosa culture, using instruments like the uhadi bow, flute, and drums to share stories and emotions. Umculo unendima ebalulekileyo kwinkcubeko yesiXhosa. Izixhobo zomculo zesintu ziquka umrhubhe, isitolotolo, nezigubhu. Iingoma zidlulisela amabali, imbali, neemvakalelo. During celebrations, ceremonies, or leisure. Ngamaxesha emibhiyozo, imisitho, okanye nje ngolonwabo.
Traditional Dances Imidaniso Yesintu
Traditional dances are vital for celebrations, expressing joy, remembrance, or culture. Imidaniso yesintu iyinxalenye ebalulekileyo yemibhiyozo nemisitho yesiXhosa. Umdaniso unokuba yindlela yokuvuyisana, yokukhumbula, okanye yokubonisa isiko. During celebrations, ceremonies, or community gatherings. Ngamaxesha emibhiyozo, imisitho, okanye iintlanganiso zoluntu.
Weaving Ukuluka
A traditional craft where people weave natural fibers into baskets, mats, or household items. Ukuluka yimisebenzi yobugcisa yesintu apho abantu benza izinto ezahlukeneyo besebenzisa imicu yendalo njengengca, iingcongolo, okanye ulusu. During leisure or as a daily task, by elders. Ngamaxesha olonwabo okanye njengomsebenzi wemihla ngemihla, ngabantu abadala.
Grinding Maize Ukuchuba Amagqabi
A traditional task where women grind maize with two stones, often singing together. Nangona ingengomdlalo, ukuchuba umbona ngamatye amabini (ilitye lokuchuba nelinye elincinci) ngumsebenzi wesintu owenziwa ngabafazi kwaye udla ngokuba yintsebenziswano apho abafazi abaninzi bechuba kunye becula iingoma. Daily, as a way to prepare food. Mihla le, njengendlela yokulungisa ukutya.
Recounting Oral History Ukubalisa Amabali Emveli
Elders pass down family and community history orally, sharing important stories. Abantu abadala banendima ebalulekileyo ekudluliseleni imbali yosapho noluntu ngomlomo. Babalisa ngamabali abalulekileyo ngeenzeko zexesha elidlulileyo kunye nezizukulwana zangaphambili. During leisure or when appropriate. Ngamaxesha olonwabo okanye xa kufanelekile.
